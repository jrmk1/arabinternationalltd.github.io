NICE TO MEET YOU!

We are committed to helping people reach their full potential and stay relevant in today’s ever-changing world of work. Let us help you find your dream job and take your career to the next level.

Arab International Ltd.(AIL) is a leading manpower recruiting company based in Bangladesh. We specialise in sending labourers, students and visitors to foreign countries like Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Dubai, Sudan, Europe and Canada for work, study and tour. Our company was founded in 1993 by Shah Jamal Khandakar who has over 30 years of experience in the recruiting industry.

His passion for helping people find jobs inspired him to start J. R. Munni Travel Agency in 1993 (Bangladesh). J. R. Munni Travel Agency was founded by him in 1993 in Bangladesh and later partnered with Al-Habib Recruite Agency (Hawali, Kuwait) and Suad Mawazi Mizban Office in Kuwait. The company was recently relaunched as “Arab International Ltd.”

At AIL, we offer a wide range of staffing services including temporary staffing, permanent staffing and contract staffing. We cover numerous industries such as construction, hospitality, healthcare and more. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing the best possible service to our clients.

We hold a Recruiting Licence (RL) from the government of Bangladesh which ensures that we are able to provide our clients with the best possible candidates. Our mission is to help people find jobs and to help companies find the right employees. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to succeed and we are committed to making that happen.
